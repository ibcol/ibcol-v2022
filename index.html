ETHICAL. PRACTICAL. IMPACTFUL.

Focusing on practical experiences and real-world impact, IBCOL inspires innovation through authentic solutions, ethical principles, and community members.

Two Competitions for Three Levels

IBCOL features two competition streams: Trust-Tech Solutions Design (DES) and Trust-Tech Solutions Engineering (ENG) for participants at secondary, tertiary, and professional levels

For Contestants

Submit your proposal in any format of your choice, such as a white-paper or slide deck.

Register by Thursday, July 31, 2025

For Observers

Secure your spot to attend the final competition as an observer. Availability is limited and offered on a first-come, first-served basis.

Register by Sunday, Aug 31, 2025.
 

For Nominators

An institution may nominate a team to participate in the final competition of the International Blockchain Olympiad. Follow-up arrangements will be directly coordinated and facilitated with the nominator.

Deadline: Sunday, Aug 31, 2025

For Partners

Apply to be an accredited partner or organizing committee

Trust Tech for Sustainable Development

Participants submit project ideas that reflect the principles of the SDGs, demonstrating their commitment to a sustainable future

IBCOL Foundation (International Blockchain Olympiad)

IBCOL is operated by an EU non-profit dedicated to addressing challenges and promoting integrity within the trust-tech industry, both in the public chain space and within regulated industries. Our mission is to advocate for practical solutions that foster a global community and culture of digital trust and innovation in trust-tech, empowering students and professionals through education and practical solutions for a sustainable future.
