IBCOL Programs

IBCOL helps students and young people learn about blockchain, cryptography, and web3. IBCOL approves educational materials and certifies courses around the world.

IBCOL seeks partners worldwide to accomplish this mission. Please contact us if you wish to contribute!

IBCOL Foundation (International Blockchain Olympiad)

We are an EU non-profit combatting bad actors and scams in the “blockchain industry”, i.e. digital trust and web3, and promoting practical solutions to foster a global community of practitioners and to empower young people through education so that they make informed career choices.
